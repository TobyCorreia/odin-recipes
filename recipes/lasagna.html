Lasagna

Description

Make this tasty lasagna for your friends and family.

Especially during cold rainy days.

Ingredients

  • 2 teaspoons dried parsley
  • 1 pound lean ground beef
  • 4 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 1 cup graded Parmesan cheese
  • 9 lasagna rolls
  • salt and pepper
  • 1 jar of tomato sauce

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 200 degrees C
  2. Cook the ground beef until brown and pour over the tomato sauce and let it stir for 5 minutes
  3. Mix the egg, Parmesan and mozzarella cheese, parsley in a bowl and add a little salt and pepper
  4. Layer the beef/tomato sauce evenly with the lasagna rolls in a baking dish and top it off with the cheese mixture
  5. Bake in the preheated oven for 45 minutes
  6. Bon appetite!
