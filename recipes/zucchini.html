Zucchini soup

Description

I love zucchini soup

Get your daily veggies with this delicious soup!

Ingredients

  • 1 onion
  • garlic
  • 2 zucchini’s
  • a litre of water
  • 2 vegetables stock cubes
  • 100 ml cream
  • olive oil

Steps

  1. Heat up a big pan and fry the onion and garlic. Add the zucchini and cook for 5 minutes
  2. Add the water and stock and let it boil for 20 minutes
  3. Puree the soup with a stick blender
  4. Don’t slurp!
