Cinnamon rolls

Description

Who doesnâ€™t love cinnamon rolls?

Make these fluffy buns in just 45 minutes!

Ingredients

  • Milk
  • Sugar
  • Flour
  • Salt
  • Egg
  • Butter
  • Cinnamon
  • Brown Sugar
  • Instant Yeast

Steps

  1. Mix the flour, sugar, salt and yeast. Stir the butter into the flour mixture and add the egg. Let it rest for a few minutes.
  2. Preheat the oven to 180 degrees C
  3. Roll the dough and sprinkle the cinnamon and brown sugar
  4. Bake the rolls for about 20 minutes until golden brown
  5. Enjoy!
